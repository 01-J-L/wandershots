## ABOUT WANDERSHOTS STUDIOS ##
Wandershots Studios is a professional photography service specializing in capturing timeless, elegant moments. Our approach combines classical composition with modern artistic vision. We have over a decade of experience.

## SERVICES OFFERED ##
1.  **On-site Studio Booth:** A premium mobile studio setup for events with professional lighting and high-quality prints.
2.  **Photobooth:** A classic photobooth experience with fun props and instant magnetic prints.
3.  **Photoman:** A roving photographer who captures candid moments at events.
4.  **Events Photography:** Full coverage for weddings, corporate events, debuts, and birthdays.
5.  **Studio Photoshoot:** Professional portrait sessions in our studio.

## PACKAGES & PRICING ##
- Our Photobooth packages start at ₱2,999.
- For Events Photography, please contact us for a custom quote as prices vary depending on the event's duration and scale.
- We offer add-ons like extra hours, custom backdrops, and guestbooks.
